# Axis Bank ACE Credit Card — Rewards Terms

## Bill Payments and Recharges

5% cashback on bill payments, mobile recharges and DTH recharges made via
Google Pay. No upper cap within the monthly cashback limit.

## Food Delivery and Cabs

4% cashback on Swiggy, Zomato and Ola. Combined monthly cashback cap of
Rs 500 across these merchants.

## All Other Spends

2% unlimited cashback on all other spends with no upper limit. Excludes
fuel, wallet loads, rent payments, jewellery and EMI transactions.

## Fees

Annual fee of Rs 499 plus taxes, waived on annual spends above Rs 2,00,000.
Interest rate of 3.75% per month on outstanding balances.
