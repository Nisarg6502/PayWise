# HDFC Bank Swiggy Credit Card — Rewards Terms

## Swiggy Spends

10% cashback on Swiggy application spends including food ordering, Instamart,
Dineout and Genie. Maximum cashback of Rs 1,500 per billing cycle on Swiggy spends.

## Online Spends

5% cashback on other online spends across all merchants. Maximum cashback of
Rs 1,500 per billing cycle on this category. Excludes rent, fuel, EMI and
wallet loads.

## Other Spends

1% cashback on all other eligible spends including offline purchases.
Maximum cashback of Rs 500 per billing cycle.

## Fees

Annual fee of Rs 500 plus taxes, waived on annual spends above Rs 2,00,000.
Finance charges of 3.6% per month (43.2% annually) on revolving credit.
